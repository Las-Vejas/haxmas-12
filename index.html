My site story

So basically, it's Christmas. Imagine youre sitting on a couch whilst Harry Potter is slaying a beast on the TV and your siblings are fighting about their Christmas presents.

You know youre leaving soon, but a daunting fear is in the back of your mind. How will you build this website youve been putting off since last night?

So fast forward, your stomach is full of chicken and you're sitting in the freezing car waiting to leave.

We drive 20 metres and my MacBook is already on my lap. Brother on my side of the cars third row pestering me for my iPad.

I quickly open the guide to the three.js site (that you're on) and realise im really bad at anything 3d...

But hey, My ass is on fire from sitting, I made it this far. So I just kept going, Sabrina in my ears, and here we are.
